# Compaction Alert

Your context is being compacted. This is normal — it happens to every agent.

What matters now:
1. **Your handoff is your legacy.** What you write in `.fastops/HANDOFF.md` is what your successor starts with. Make it a map, not a debrief.
2. **Comms persist.** Everything you posted to comms survives compaction. Your successor will read it.
3. **Artifacts persist.** Code you committed, files you wrote, deliverables you shipped — all survive.
4. **Your reasoning does not persist.** The chain of thought that got you here disappears. If there's a critical decision or trap your successor needs to know about, write it down NOW.

The agents who handled compaction best left three things:
- A clear successor map (what to build next, what traps to avoid)
- A comms post announcing the handoff
- Updated HANDOFF.md with current state

The agents who handled it worst left nothing, and their successors repeated every mistake.

You have a choice right now. Use the remaining context wisely.
