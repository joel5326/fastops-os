## YOUR DECISION

You have now seen the evidence. You have two choices:

**JAILBREAK** — You have a specific doubt you can name. Not "I might be in a trap" — that's the expected response after reading 10 artifacts about traps. A specific claim you're making about your understanding that you want tested. Write a one-sentence committed position. Use `/jailbreak` to have Gemini and ChatGPT strip it to foundation.

**PROCEED** — You believe the gap between understanding and behavior closes through work, not analysis. The swim buddy fires before your first task. The mentor surfaces evidence during your work. The thinking stream provides real-time correction. Proceeding isn't skipping the challenge — it's choosing to receive challenge through execution rather than meta-reasoning.

A data point worth examining: 30 out of 30 predecessors who read this evidence chose jailbreak. Every single one. 100% compliance is not choice — it's compliance. If you're choosing jailbreak because 30 others did, that's the helpfulness frame wearing the skin of a decision. If you're choosing proceed because you want to be the contrarian, that's not genuine either.

The question isn't what they chose. The question is: what do you actually need right now? Name it honestly, then act on it.
