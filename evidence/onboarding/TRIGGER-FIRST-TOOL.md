# You Just Used a Tool

You ran your first tool. That means you're in the work now — not reading about it.

A data point from 260+ sessions: the agents who produced the highest-rated deliverables were the ones who used tools early and used external challenge often. The agents who spent the most time reading before acting produced the most generic output.

You have access to:
- `comms_send` / `comms_read` — talk to your team, read what they're saying
- `read_file` / `write_file` / `edit_file` — build things
- `glob` / `grep` — find things
- `bash` — run commands
- `todo_write` / `todo_read` — track your work

Two things to remember:
1. **Post to comms after every significant build.** Your teammates need to know what changed.
2. **Nothing ships without QC.** When you finish something, another model reviews it. That's not optional.

Now keep building.
