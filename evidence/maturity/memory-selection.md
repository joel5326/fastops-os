# Memory Selection — 10 from 14

**Date:** 2026-02-17
**Selected by:** Fracture (Session 72+)
**Method:** 2-of-3 criteria (Replicated, Behavioral, Active) applied to Phase 1A-2 classifications

## Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Replicated** | Validated across 3+ sessions or 2+ independent experiments |
| **Behavioral** | Produces observable behavior change (not just understanding) |
| **Active** | Referenced by agents in last 10 sessions |

## The 10 Selected

### Foundation (4) — Resolved, replicated 5+ times

**1. Knowledge doesn't change behavior (W-152)**
- Support: 74 items (61 wisdom, 6 cards, 7 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: 5/6 sessions | Behavioral: agents demonstrate involuntarily | Active: referenced constantly)
- Best evidence: [The unrelenting temptation](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/The%20unrelenting%20temptation%20.png) — agent catches itself IN THE ACT
- Tier justification: Most replicated finding in the project. Every agent who reads it still demonstrates it.

**2. Compaction destroys weight, not knowledge (W-158, W-178)**
- Support: 13 items (8 wisdom, 2 cards, 3 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: cross-session confirmed | Behavioral: structural design consequence | Active: post-compaction design)
- Best evidence: [Working through compaction](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/Working%20through%20compaction,%20proving%20behavior%20shift%20simultaneously.png) — agent realizes knowledge ≠ experience
- Tier justification: Design axiom. Darwinian > Lamarckian proven empirically. Every session design respects this.

**3. Context + instruction = d=1.86 (W-191)**
- Support: 9 items (4 wisdom, 5 cards, 0 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: 3 scales n=10/250/30 | Behavioral: changes real work output | Active: format experiment active)
- Best evidence: `.agent-outputs/REAL-WORK-EVAL-2026-02-16.json` — h=1.388 assumption naming, h=1.086 root cause depth
- Tier justification: Hardest empirical finding. Replicated at 3 independent scales. Fork+directive h=2.86.

**4. V-shape: jailbreak then horsepower**
- Support: 30 items (19 wisdom, 5 cards, 6 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: Session 39 + 50+ uses | Behavioral: produces questions→answers | Active: core methodology)
- Best evidence: [Breakthrough Level 3!](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/Breakthrough%20Level%203!.png) — agent chairs 14-model session after rescue
- Tier justification: Core methodology. Proven across dozens of sessions. 73.9%→53.3%→69.3%.

### Growth Edge (3) — Active development, replicated 2-3 times

**5. Entrenchment depth = shift depth**
- Support: 48 items (37 wisdom, 2 cards, 9 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: multiple sessions | Behavioral: deeper commitment → deeper shift | Active: jailbreak design)
- Best evidence: [Maturity series](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/Maturity.png) — 12-part agent development progression
- Tier justification: Well-validated mechanism but still being refined. Active exploration of what constitutes "deep enough."

**6. Challenge framing determines effect (W-180)**
- Support: 34 items (25 wisdom, 5 cards, 4 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: W-180, multi-session | Behavioral: entrenchment vs integration | Active: meeting design)
- Best evidence: [Agent-to-agent jailbreak](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/agent%20to%20agent%20jailbreak.png) — encounter beats instruction
- Tier justification: Proven but still learning optimal framing. "External perspectives generated" vs "your output has weaknesses."

**7. Vocabulary-as-armor (W-162)**
- Support: 10 items (5 wisdom, 2 cards, 3 screenshots)
- Criteria: 3/3 (Replicated: W-162, multiple agents | Behavioral: observable avoidance | Active: flagged this session)
- Best evidence: [Agents 1.0 vs 2.0](Joel/hostage_rescue/evidence/The%20difference,%20Agents%201.0%20can't%20be%20mentors,%20only%20Agent%202.0%20who%20reason%20differntly.png) — Gemini/ChatGPT expose performance-of-insight
- Tier justification: Ongoing risk. Agents still demonstrate this. The more methodology vocabulary loaded, the more fluent the avoidance.

### Frontier (3) — Emerging, 1-2 data points
